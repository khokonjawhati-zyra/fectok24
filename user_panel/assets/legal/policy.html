Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 16, 2026

SOVEREIGN V15 ARCHITECTURE: This application operates on the Sovereign Decentralized Mesh Protocol. Your data is cryptographically secured. The Administrator (Architect) acts as a passive node facilitator and does not exercise direct ownership over user-generated content (UGC).

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you create an account, upload content, or interact with other users. This includes:

  • Mesh Identity: Your unique SOV_ID, username, and bio.
  • Media Content: Videos, images, and audio you upload to the Mesh.
  • Transactional Data: Ledger entries for internal asset exchanges (COINS, USD, BDT).
  • Technical Logs: IP address, device type, and app interaction metadata (A_106 Evidence Archiving).

2. How We Use Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Operate and maintain the Sovereign V15 Mesh ecosystem.
  • Ensure system security and prevent fraudulent activity.
  • Comply with legal requirements and our Community Guidelines.
  • Provide real-time synchronization across nodes.

3. Data Deletion & Retention

In compliance with Google Play Policies, users have the absolute right to request the deletion of their accounts and associated data. You can initiate this process through Settings > Delete Account within the application. Upon confirmation, your Mesh Identity will be purged from the central manifesto, and your media references will be de-linked.

4. User Content Liability

As per the Sovereign Statute, users bear sole legal responsibility for the content they upload. The Admin is not liable for copyright infringements or illegal acts perpetrated by users. We provide reporting tools for the community to flag violations for manual review.

5. Security

All data is transmitted via secure WebSocket layers (WSS) and stored with industry-standard encryption. However, users are responsible for securing their Transaction PINs and Device Access.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may evolve this policy as the Mesh grows. Continued use of the application constitutes acceptance of the latest revisions.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or data security on the Mesh, contact the Sovereign Support Node at: support@lovetok.sovereign
